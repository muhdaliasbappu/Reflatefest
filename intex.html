About

REFLATE, The Festival Of Knowledge, is an annual National Level Economics fest of the Department of Economics, St Joseph’s College (Autonomous) Devagiri, which provides students from across the country a platform to combine economics learning with competing and creative expression, Through our package of exhilarating events that include Quizzes, paper presentations, debates, simulation games and guest lectures we seek to evince interest in economics among young minds .Through the years, Reflate has grown in popularity and participation, Reflate has seen participants from reputed institutions from all over India. On account of covid pandemic , This year we have chosen to go hybrid with both online and offline events. This year we bring to you four highly interesting events for Reflate 2022 , Zero Hour(Quiz), Oikonomia(Paper presentation, Symposium(Debate), The Social Startup ((Social Business Plan Competition) are designed with fun and learning in mind. Economics @ St Joseph College, Devagiri Department of Economics at St Joseph College (Autonomous), Devagiri has a long-cherished history; Set up in the year 1956, this department offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in Economics. The department has been consistently producing excellent results at undergraduate and postgraduate graduate levels examinations. The dynamic and highly committed teaching faculty and a very vibrant group of students make it one of the most desired departments for economic education in the state. The alumni of the department hold prominent positions in academics and administrative spheres across the state and the country. The department regularly organises a variety of departmental activities like guest lectures, research workshops and fests to encourage students to participate, explore and learn by active engagement and interaction with experts in concerned areas providing a creative environment to enhance their knowledge and skills for life beyond college.
